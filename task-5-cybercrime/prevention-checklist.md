# Part B: Stay Safe Online Checklist

**File Name**: prevention-checklist.md (Save in task-5-cybercrime/ folder)

**UPI PIN Rule** (Financial): You should never enter your UPI PIN to get money. You only need to enter it to send or pay money.

**Verify QR Codes** (Financial): You must treat QR codes like cash. Never scan a QR code from someone you do not know.

**Official Reporting**: If you think someone has cheated you report it away at the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in). You can also call the 1930 helpline.

**Enable 2FA**: You should use Two-Factor Authentication on your college email and social media accounts. This will make them more secure.

**Check URLs**: Always check that a websites URL is correct. Look for https:// before you enter your login details.

**Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking**: Never log into your bank accounts or UPI apps when you are using Wi-Fi.

**Privacy Settings**: Keep your social media profiles private. This will stop people from stealing your photos and data.

**Beware of "Urgent" Requests**: Be careful, with messages that try to make you feel scared or worried. This is a trick that scammers use to get your information.
