#FIRST TASK(INFOGRAPHIC)
For the first task, I had to create an infographic for creating awareness about digital literacy. 
I chose canva which is a popular, user-friendly online graphic design platform that allows users to create social media graphics, presentations, posters, documents, and other visual content. It is a very convenient and free to use tool to a great extent, you can easily get pre-made templates and objects along with assisting image generation which makes the graphic look more appealing. 

I divided this infographic into 4-major parts or topics as to increase readability. Firstly, **Digital literacy** which is ability to use digital devices effectively,
Safely engaging in exchange of communication and financial transactions, Understanding use of different online platforms. 

Secondly, **Online Security** which is Identifying common online risks such as Phishing, UPI fraud, and Identity Theft, Practicing "Stay Safe Online" habits, like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links, Knowing how to use official channels like the National Cyber Crime Portal (1930 helpline) if a fraud occurs.

Third, **Professional Presence** which tells to Establish professional profiles on Linkedin, GitHub, and Kaggle to showcase your education and specific learning goals,
Post Precise and to-the-point content to connect better with others and to create a professional look use formal and easy to read language.

Fourth, **Email Etiquette** which tells to Master respectful, well-structured email formats.
Write concise and to the point emails for better readability, Ensure the email includes proper subject-line and ensure proper grammar is used using Al-tools.

While creating this infographic, the most challenging part was to arrange all the points in order, be concise about it as the space was limited and making the format and the identation similar to other parts to make the infographic look neat and clean. Thankfully, Canva has different kind of measurement mark, shapes & elements and different kinds of tools to easily arrange information and graphics. I found it incredibly interesting how you can make such good and professional looking graphics on an online website that too for no cost.
