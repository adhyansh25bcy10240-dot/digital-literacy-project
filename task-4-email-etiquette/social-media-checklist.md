# Social Media Do’s:


**1. Do keep your profile professional by using a clear profile picture and accurate bio.**


**2. Do think twice before posting; ensure your content wouldn't embarrass you in front of a future employer.**


**3. Do use privacy settings to control who can see your personal information.**


**4. Do engage in positive and constructive discussions that add value to your community.**


**5. Do verify information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.**

# Social Media Don'ts:


**1. Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or hateful comments.**


**2. Don't share sensitive personal details like your home address, phone number, or OTPs.**


**3. Don't engage with "trolls" or participate in cyberbullying.**


**4. 't use unprofessional language or slang that could damage your reputation.**


**5. Don't tag others in photos or posts without their explicit permission.**
